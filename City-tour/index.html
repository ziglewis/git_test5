Visit the Enyimba City of Aba

Enjoy the ever fun city of Aba in Abia State, Nigeria.

A city that has something for everyone no matter your passion.

Top three places to visit in Aba, the Enyimba City!

Watch a live football match

The Enyimba stadium also called the Peoples Elephants Stadium is a place to visit by all football lovers

Buy whatsoever you desire in this market and meet diverse skilled men and women

Ariaria International market is arguably one of the largest market in the whole of West Africa and is predominantly known for her leather works especially shoes and bags.

Relax and unpack in this 5-star hotel located in the GRA

Ibiza hotel is a cool place to unpack while touring in Aba, owing to its accessibility, facilities and central location.
